Interpretation

  • Below -0.3 indicates increasingly corrosive water.
  • Above +0.3 indicates increasing scale-forming tendency.
  • LSI is an equilibrium indicator, not a complete corrosion assessment.

Method

LSI = measured pH − saturation pH. CYA correction uses one-third of measured CYA as an operational approximation.
